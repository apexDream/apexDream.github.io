    About

    I am an aspiring Game Engineer with a passion for creating immersive experiences in the world of XR (Extended Reality). My journey in the field began in August 2021 when I took on the role of Tutor and Teacher Assistant at Abilene Christian University. During this time, I had the privilege of assisting fellow students in mastering various software programs, including Unity, Blender, Unreal, Maya, and Photoshop. As a TA, I also played a crucial role in organizing and enhancing student projects to ensure they achieved the highest level of usability and quality.

    Having developed a strong foundation through my academic pursuits, I successfully completed my Bachelor of Science in Digital Entertainment from Abilene Christian University in May 2023. During my time at the university, I honed my skills and knowledge to become a proficient and versatile professional in the gaming industry.

    With a keen interest in AI and its potential to revolutionize gaming experiences, I delved into exploring Artificial Intelligence in the context of game development. Through self-guided learning and coursework, I gained valuable insights into various AI techniques, such as pathfinding algorithms, behavior trees, and decision-making systems. This knowledge not only enriched my understanding of AI but also allowed me to integrate intelligent NPCs (Non-Playable Characters) and enemies into the games I worked on, providing players with challenging and dynamic gameplay experiences.

    In December 2022, I embarked on an exciting internship at The Common XR - Link, where I held the position of Debugger. As part of my responsibilities, I worked closely with my lead to debug code and address any issues that arose in the Unity experience's data connection to the database. My keen problem-solving abilities allowed me to find effective solutions to various challenges, ensuring a smooth user experience.

    During my internship, I took initiative to improve the debugging process further. I implemented new Debugger tools, which enabled me to identify objects based on their UUID (Universally Unique Identifier), streamlining the debugging workflow and increasing efficiency.

    In addition to debugging, I actively contributed to gameplay mechanics during my internship. I collaborated with the development team to design and implement innovative gameplay features, ensuring a captivating and enjoyable user experience. Leveraging my knowledge of AI, I integrated intelligent enemy behaviors, dynamic world interactions, and engaging mission structures that kept players immersed and entertained.

    To bolster my expertise, I have earned certifications as a Unity Certified Associate in both Game Development (UCA) and Artistry (UCAA). These certifications demonstrate my dedication to mastering the Unity engine and my commitment to delivering top-notch game experiences.

    With a blend of academic knowledge, hands-on experience, and a creative mindset, I am enthusiastic about continuing my journey in the gaming industry. I am constantly seeking opportunities to collaborate with like-minded professionals, pushing the boundaries of innovation, AI-driven gameplay, and creating remarkable experiences in the world of XR.
